BA Case Study

Integrated Safety Intelligence Framework

This case demonstrates how safety surveillance data, programme outcomes, and frontline insight can be integrated to improve decision-making.

Problem

Safety data and programme data often exist in silos, delaying insight.

Approach

  • Stakeholder mapping
  • Process analysis (As-Is / To-Be)
  • Requirements definition
  • Risk & benefits assessment

Outcome

Improved visibility of safety trends and programme performance.
